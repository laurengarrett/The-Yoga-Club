Welcome to
The Yoga Group!

We are a community of people concered with improving and maintaining our mental, physical, sprititual, and social health in part through the practice of yoga.

Here you will find a very diverse group of practicers, from young to old, seasoned yogi to a brand-new student. Whoever you are, you are welcome here.

As a member, you have first access to our many exciting and invigorating events, including yoga classes and retreats. We practice all accross the United States, so you are sure to find a nearby group.
